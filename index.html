Advanced Technology

5-Stage Advanced
Water Purification

Every drop undergoes rigorous multi-stage treatment for guaranteed purity

01
⚡
Pre-Ozonation
Ozone eliminates bacteria, viruses and harmful germs before filtration begins.
02
🪨
Sand Filtration
Removes dirt, sand and visible particles for crystal-clear water.
03
🫧
Activated Carbon
Eliminates chlorine, odour, bad taste and harmful chemical residues.
04
💧
Reverse Osmosis
Removes salts, heavy metals and micro-impurities. Ultra-low TDS.
05
☀️
UV Disinfection
UV light destroys any remaining germs, ensuring 100% safe drinking water.
